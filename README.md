# Retail-Sales-performance-Analysis-
Retail Sales performance Analysis using Microsoft Excel and PowerBI 

Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard

Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales performance using interactive dashboards in Power BI.
The objective is to track revenue trends, evaluate product performance, and generate insights to support business decision-making.

---
Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profit performance
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Compare sales across regions
- Evaluate sales channels and trends over time

---

Tools Used

- Power BI
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data Visualization

---
Dashboard Features

Key KPIs

- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin (%)
- Total Units Sold

---

Key Visuals

- Revenue Trend Over Time
- Profit by Sales Channel
- Revenue by Product Category
- Revenue by Region
- Category-wise Profit Comparison

---
Key Business Insights

- The South region generates the highest revenue among all regions
- Store sales channel produces more profit compared to online sales
- Electronics category contributes the largest share of total revenue
- Revenue shows fluctuations but generally declines toward the later days of the month

---

Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions to maximize revenue
- Improve online sales performance to match store profitability
- Expand high-performing categories like electronics
- Address declining sales trends by introducing mid-month campaigns or promotions
